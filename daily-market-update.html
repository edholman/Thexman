Market Commentary

What today's numbers mean for you
Rates Trending Down

Mortgage rates have declined across the board today, driven by recent economic data suggesting a cooling job market. This presents a favorable window for borrowers considering a purchase or refinance.

What This Means for You:

  • First-Time Buyers: Lower rates improve affordability and monthly payment options
  • Refinancers: If rates are 0.5% or more below your current rate, it's time to talk
  • Investors: Better cash flow potential with reduced debt service on rental properties
Remember: Rates can change throughout the day. These are indicative rates for well-qualified borrowers. Your actual rate depends on credit, loan amount, property type, and other factors.
